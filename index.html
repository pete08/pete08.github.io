About Me

Growing up I have always been fascinated by tech and how it changes our lives. From an early age I jumped into HTML courses and graphic designing. Through scratching the web development surface I gained a miniscual amount of knowledge and insight into the prolific tech industry. I've grown to realize the tech industry compliments my natural creativity, passions, and curiousity.


Professionally trained as an accountant I went from tax to external auditing to SOX compliance audits to internal SOX and finally Operational audit. I was under the impression this was it, not too excited about the month-to-month and pretty even-keel projects.


A couple years ago my audit department started an automation initiative and was able to take the necessary steps to involve myself and learn the basics. I got my feet wet with automation and Python and recently jumped all in completing a programming bootcamp.
